StoreBioinfo

sustainable storage to the Norwegian life sciences community

StoreBioinfoGUI is a front end to StoreBioinfo making it straightforward to organize the data in the format required by StoreBioinfo. It also makes sure that the data has the meta data needed for future re-use.

Project home page: http://store-bioinfo-gui.googlecode.com.

For more details about StoreBioinfo see:
http://www.bioinfo.no/storebioinfo and
https://storebioinfo.norstore.no.
